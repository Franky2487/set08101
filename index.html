The Last Viking Hero

"The Last Viking King Game" tells of Ragnar and his many raids against British isles and Holy Roman Empire.

Ragnar was a boy when his father, King of Sweeden, was busy protecting his people and his resources against raids of the barbarian tribe on the land.

Ragnar, since he was a child, he wanted to know what there was over his lands.

Until the day Ragnar became a strong man and with his companions start to build a big ship. The shipbuilder is another famous Viking in History, and his name is Floki. He was the first Viking to discover Iceland.

With his companions and then famous Great Heathen Army, Ragnar invaded the actual UK in 865 AD.

He conquered the four English Kingdom. After this invasion, Ragnar went back to Scandinavia. He returned as a god, and people cheer him. After two months in the 891 AD, Ragnar got married to Erin, an Irish princess. They had three sons and on the evening of 25 December 893, Ragnar was declared King of England and Scandinavia after a long raids life.
